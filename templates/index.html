FAQ

See our most common questions

What if i don't want to buy any plan

Don’t worry, we allow every user to post up to 50 posts and have up to 2,000 subscribers to give you plenty of time to test the app. Once you reach the post limit, you won’t be able to create additional posts, but rest assured, we won’t block new subscribers from joining. We’ll send you a notification in advance so you have time to adjust.

The Enterprise plan doesn't fit my needs

In that case, please contact us to create you a special al tailored plan to your needs

I exceed my plan, what's next?

We won’t ban you or block your subscribers' flow. If you exceed your plan limits, we’ll notify you and give you time to adjust your plan. If there’s no response within 3 months, we’ll send a final notice that in one week, we’ll have to pause your app’s functionality. However, your posts will remain online permanently unless you choose to delete them.

Do you offer plans for non-profit?

Yes, absolutely! We offer special plans for non-profit organizations. Feel free to reach out to us to learn more about the options available.

I want to change my user ID, can i?

No, your user ID is unique and cannot be changed. If you accidentally choose an offensive or inappropriate ID, please contact us, and we’ll assist you in resolving the issue.

What if i want to post with a photo from my computer?

Since we don’t support file uploads, you can either use images from the internet or upload your photos to an external service. We’ve designed it this way to keep the service affordable and accessible.
